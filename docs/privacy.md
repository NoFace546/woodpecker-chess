# Privacy Policy — Woodpecker Chess

**Last updated:** 2026-04-29

Woodpecker Chess ("the app") is a chess training application created by Jed Sebios.
This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

## Data we collect

**None.** The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with us
or any third party.

## Data stored on your device

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**, in its private
application storage:

- The puzzle library bundled with the app (Lichess open puzzle dataset, CC0).
- Your puzzle attempts, sets, rounds, and Elo rating.
- Your bot game state.
- App preferences (sound mute, theme).

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it via the
"Export data" feature, which uses your device's share sheet to send the file
wherever you direct it (e.g. Google Drive, email, local files).

## No analytics, no telemetry

The app does not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, or any
other analytics, telemetry, or crash-reporting service. No usage data is sent
anywhere.

## Network usage

The app does not make network requests during normal operation. The Stockfish
chess engine runs entirely on-device.

## Third-party content

- **Puzzles**: from the Lichess open puzzle database (CC0).
- **Chess engine**: Stockfish (GPL-3.0).
- **Chess UI libraries**: chessground and dartchess by Lichess (GPL-3.0).

These are bundled with the app and do not communicate with their authors' servers.

## Permissions

The app requests no special Android permissions beyond what's required for basic
functionality (vibration, internet — used only by Android system, not by the app
itself).

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will replace the version at this URL.
The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the change.
